Our Mission

Guardians of The Amazon

Headquartered in Manaus, Brazil, we are at the frontlines of defending the world's most vital ecosystem. We believe the rainforest and its native inhabitants must be protected at all costs.

Led by our Chief Conservation Officer, Elena Costa, our organization combats illegal logging, restores deforested lands, and empowers indigenous communities with legal defense and resources. Whether we are tracking deforestation via satellite or patrolling native reserves, our commitment is unwavering.
